Help

Who?

Spanish Ruby User Group

What?

EuRuKo 2009, the annual Eurupean Ruby Conference is hosted in a different city every year, this year will be Barcelona. It's a nice opportunity to meet other collegues and get uptodate with the european ruby community in an informal environment.

Where?

This event will be held in Barcelona, on the Citilab.

Pl. Can Suris, s/n
08940 Cornellà de Llobregat (BCN)
Spain
map

How to get there?

Just follow the instuctions, step by step :).

When?

May 9th and 10th, 2009.

How much does it cost?

30 Euros, taxes included.

How do I register?

You need to register as a user on registration application and then go to the "Pay" section. Follow the steps from there to select the event and pay through PayPal, or credit card.

Being registered on the application doesn'tn imply you have a seat, only when your payment has been received, your seat will be reserved for you.

Right now we're fully booked and we can't sell any more tickets for the event. But if you still want to come, there is a ticket available for free from one of our sponsors, Ameets.com http://blog.ameets.com/2009/05/ameets-patrocina-euroko-2009/ (contact jose.attala@ameets.com )

Invoices.

Invoices will be sent by e-mail.

Please if you need the invoice of the EuRuKo 2009 ticket send us an email to invoices@srug.es.

It's important that you check the "Invoice Details" section on your user profile on registration application, as this is what will appear on the invoice..
