Follow the EuRuKo bot!

Posted by Jaime Iniesta on May 08, 2009 at 02:42 PM

We've set up a little twitter bot that will be constantly scanning the #euruko hashtag feed and retwitting them, so it's easy for everyone to be up to date on what's been twitted about the EuRuKo conference.

You can find the EuRuKo twitter bot here:

http://twitter.com/euruko_bot

Please remember to include the #euruko hashtag on all your twitts about the conference!

Thanks to the Scotland on Rails team and specially to Mark Conell for sharing this script with us!
