Thank you all

Posted by Javier Ramírez on May 11, 2009 at 09:55 AM

We would like to give a big thank you to everybody attending EuRuKo 2009 for their enthusiasm, to the speakers for the great work they did and to our sponsors for making all of this possible.

If you were taking pictures and you would like to share them, don't forget to tag them in flickr like this http://www.flickr.com/photos/tags/euruko2009/

It was a pleasure to be hosting the conference this year, and we would like to congratulate the fantastic representation of Polish rubyists on the opportunity to hold the event next time (gratulacje!). After meeting them in Barcelona, we are sure EuRuKo 2010 is going to be a big success.

See you all in Kraków!

What's EuRuKo?

EuRuKo is an annual conference about the Ruby programming language with an informal atmosphere and lots of opportunities to listen, to talk, to hack and to have fun. This year takes place on Barcelona, Spain, the days 9th and 10th of May.
